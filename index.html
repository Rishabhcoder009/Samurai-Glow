⚔️ 5 Phase Transformation Plan (100–120kg Safe Progression)

Phase 1 – After Boards (4 Weeks)

  • 30–40 min Brisk Walking (5 days)
  • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x12
  • Chair Squats – 3x12
  • Resistance Band Rows – 3x15
  • Glute Bridges – 3x15
  • Core Brace Hold – 20 sec x 3
  • 2 Rest Days

Goal: Reduce fat safely, strengthen joints.

Phase 2 – School Start (1 Month)

  • 3 Walking Days (30 min)
  • Leg Press – 3x12
  • Machine Chest Press – 3x12
  • Seated Rows – 3x12
  • Low Step-ups – 3x10 each leg
  • 2 Rest Days

Goal: Maintain progress with school schedule.

Phase 3 – 1.5 Month Break

  • 45 min Fast Walking OR Cycling (4 days)
  • Full Body Machine Workout (3 days)
  • Leg Press – 4x12
  • Chest Press – 4x12
  • Lat Pulldown – 4x12
  • Seated Shoulder Press – 3x12
  • Seated Knee Raises – 3x12
  • 1 Rest Day

Goal: Strong fat loss + muscle build safely.

Phase 4 – Regular School Routine

  • 3 Days 30–40 min Walking
  • 2–3 Days Strength Training
  • 1 Light Sport / Cycling
  • 2 Rest Days

Goal: Sustain weight loss and stay consistent.

⚔️ Phase 5 – Build My Physique

Move here when walking feels easy and joints feel strong.

Push Day

  • Chest Press – 4x10
  • Seated Shoulder Press – 4x10
  • Tricep Pushdown – 3x12

Pull Day

  • Lat Pulldown – 4x10
  • Seated Row – 4x12
  • Dumbbell Curl – 3x12

Leg Day

  • Leg Press – 4x12
  • Hamstring Curl – 3x12
  • Glute Bridge – 3x15

Goal: Body Recomposition — Lose fat + Build muscle + Improve strength.
